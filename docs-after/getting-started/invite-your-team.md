# Invite your team

<div align="left"><figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Card-11.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

## How to Invite Users <a href="#how-to-invite-users" id="how-to-invite-users"></a>

{% hint style="warning" %}
Only admins can invite new users and assign roles. Be sure to select the appropriate role based on the level of access each team member needs.
{% endhint %}

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
### Access Team Management

Navigate to **Settings → Organization → Team Management** (admin access required).
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Send Invitations

Click **Invite Team Member** and provide:

* Email address
* Role (Admin, Finance, Operations, or Developer)
* Department (optional)
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Invitation Process

The invited user will receive an email with:

* A link to create their account
* Information about their assigned role
* Instructions for first-time login

{% hint style="info" %}
Team invitations are typically delivered within a few minutes and expire after **7 days** if not accepted.
{% endhint %}
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}

## Managing Team Members <a href="#managing-team-members" id="managing-team-members"></a>

### **Edit User Permissions**

Click on any team member to:

* Change their role or department
* Update contact information
* Reset their password
* Deactivate their account

### **Remove Users**

Deactivate team members who no longer need access. Their historical activity remains visible for audit purposes.
